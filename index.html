Why this exists
Loan tracking apps get it wrong.
What other trackers do
  • Split EMI into interest + principal using textbook formula
  • Calculate interest monthly, not daily
  • Don't match your actual bank statement
  • Require you to create an account and share data
  • Show you ads or sell your data
What GrihaSetu does
  • Full EMI reduces outstanding immediately — interest is a separate month-end charge
  • Daily diminishing balance — the actual bank method
  • Numbers match your bank statement exactly
  • No account, no server, no tracking — runs entirely in your browser
  • Free forever, no ads, your data stays in your Drive
Get started in 15 minutes
One-time setup, then forget about it.
01
Host the app on GitHub (free)
Create a free GitHub account, upload the HTML file, enable Pages. You get a permanent URL you can open on any device. Takes 5 minutes.
02
Connect Google Drive (free)
Register a free Google Cloud project, get an OAuth Client ID, paste it into the file. This lets the app save your data to your own Drive. One-time, 10 minutes.
03
Enter your loan details
Open the app, go to Loan Setup, enter your loan amount, rate, EMI, and start date. Add your past transactions from your bank statement if you want a complete history.
04
Use it on your EMI date every month
When your EMI hits, open the app, click "Auto-fill EMI", and add the entry. At month-end, add the interest charge from your bank statement. That's it — everything updates automatically.
